# [Marketing Plan] Shout Launch & Growth Strategy

## 1. Target Audience
- **Primary**: Young professionals and university students (aged 18–34) living in shared housing or social circles in Brisbane (primarily around Fortitude Valley and West End).
- **Key Use Case**: Splitting group bills at pubs, breweries, and shared dining tables.

---

## 2. Pub & Venue Partnership Plan
- **Launch Partner**: *Felons Brewing Co.* (Story Bridge, Brisbane).
- **Integration**:
  - QR codes on dining tables linking directly to Shout webapp room creation.
  - "It's your shout!" weekend promotions: users get a 5% discount on the total bill if split and paid instantly via Shout.

---

## 3. Viral Growth Loops (Referral Program)
- **Referral Benefit**: Share a custom link. If a friend signs up and completes their first split payment, both receive $5 credit towards their next "shout".
- **Social Sharing**: One-click sharing of receipt splits to Instagram Stories and group chats.

---

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Target under $2.50 AUD.
- **Monthly Active Users (MAU)**: Target 5,000 by Q3.
- **Retention Rate (Week 4)**: Target > 30% repeat bill splits.
